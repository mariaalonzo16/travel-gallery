Why I'm Famous?

It is the most important modern tourist destination in the city of Valencia and one of the 12 Treasures of Spain. The City of Arts and Sciences is situated at the southeast end of the former riverbed of the river Turia, which was drained and rerouted after a catastrophic flood in 1957.

Cost

General Admission is 7.95 euro (9 US Dollars).

Mediterraneo Pizza

Burger Dolphinarium

Located under the bleachers of the Dolphinarium, allows you to taste a delicious hamburger before or after enjoying the exhibition of dolphins.

Why I'm Famous?

Located in the avant-garde architectural complex of the City of Arts and Sciences in Valencia, the aquarium is a true tribute to the seas and oceans of the planet and contains large aquariums which faithfully reproduce the most important marine ecosystems. A whole sea of sensations which can spark our imagination.

Cost

General Admission is $35.

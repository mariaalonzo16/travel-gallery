About

It is the most important modern tourist destination in the city of Valencia and one of the 12 Treasures of Spain. The City of Arts and Sciences is situated at the southeast end of the former riverbed of the river Turia, which was drained and rerouted after a catastrophic flood in 1957.

Cost

General Admission is 7.95 euro (9 US Dollars).

Places to Eat Nearby

OCEANS RESTAURANT

The ideal place to enjoy a varied choice of meat and products of the Mediterranean (grilled meats, fritters, burgers, etc.) to taste both in its hall as in its large terrace of wood.

Next to the impressive aviary is the terrace where you can enjoy the spectacular views of the wetlands while enjoying a delicious ice cream or granita. In addition, offers pizzas, sandwiches and waffles craftsmen to replace forces at any time.

BURGER DOLPHINARIUM

About

Located in the avant-garde architectural complex of the City of Arts and Sciences in Valencia, the aquarium is a true tribute to the seas and oceans of the planet and contains large aquariums which faithfully reproduce the most important marine ecosystems. A whole sea of sensations which can spark our imagination.

Cost

General Admission is $35.

This lively Old Town square is a great place for people-watching and taking photos of some of Valencia's most emblematic buildings. Visit Plaza de la Virgen to watch the daily rhythms of Valéncian life, take photographs of historic city buildings and enjoy cultural celebrations.

Places to Eat Nearby

CRÊPERIE BRETONNE ANNAICK

Hidden in a small alleyway next to the cathedral you’ll find this café which serves high-quality, traditional crêpes.

SAONA

With a Balearic decor and nature accents, Saona is a modern lounge situated at Plaza de la Virgen. The modern lounge serves amongst others delicious chicken teriyaki, patatas bravas and salads.

A yummy brunch and breakfast restaurant at the corner of the lively plaza, so you can enjoy your food with a beautiful view. Just by taking a look at the pictures, your mouth will already start to water. Your appetite will be filled with delicious smoothies, salads and pancakes!
